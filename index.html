Tokenomics

BSC Contract Address: 0x1440270b79bc1516a75f7f9b84c87afa30302717

Liquidity Locked, Renounced Ownership, deflationary (a portion of every transaction is burned), and fair launched.

GatesSorosJones2049Inu is a BEP-20 token running on the Binance Smart Chain Network. It functions similarly to other BEP-20 tokens, however the transfer function within the smart contract makes it unique (e.g., reflection and liquidity pool acquisition.)

All token transfers incur a 10% fee.

5% goes towards reflections, which is where holders passively receive rewards in proportion to their token holdings based on an individual address.

5% goes towards liquidity pool acquisition. This fee is broken down to 2.5% NWO and 2.5% BNB which provides additional liquidity and overall health of the Pancake Swap NWO/BNB trading pair.
